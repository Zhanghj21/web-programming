Rudiger will leave Chelsea this summer

Romano confirmed that Rudiger will leave Chelsea this summer. He is not willing to wait another two months for the arrival of the new boss when his contract expires. At present, Chelsea have not yet made an offer to renew their contract. Rudiger has always respected the club, but now he has to meet new challenges.
